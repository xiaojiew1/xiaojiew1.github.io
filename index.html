I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate at University of Melbourne (the expected graduation date is between Nov 2019 and May 2020). My research interests lie in search and recommendation systems. I received both a B.S. degree in applied mathematics and a B.E. degree in computer science from Renmin University of China in 2016. I work closely with Prof. Zhicheng Dou at Renmin University of China.
